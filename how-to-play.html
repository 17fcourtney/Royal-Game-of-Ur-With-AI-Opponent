HOW TO PLAY

START

The game is played in turns. Each turn, a player rolls four dice shaped as triangular pyramids.

For each painted point facing up of a dice rolled, adds to how many spaces are given to move whichever piece they like.

The player then chooses which piece to move.

MOVEMENT

The player can only move to a space if...
  • A space is not occupied
  • A space is occupied by an opponent and is not a rossette
If the piece is moved to a rossette space, the player gets another roll.

When a player moves to a space that is occupied by the opponent, the opponent's piece is then moved back to the starting position.

If a player can not move, or the player rolled a 0, their turn is over.

WINNING

Once a player has all 5 pieces off the board, they win.
